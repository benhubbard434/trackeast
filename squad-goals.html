Squad Goals

Squad goals is changing. Back in 2020, when Government restrictions first eased, we set up groups of 6 to encourage social contact still to happen with the crew. These were set up to minimise travel, and set up running bublles whilst respecting Government guidelines.

We're asking people to sign up again as we're aware lots of people have moved, and we want to change the group sizes a little to encourage more runners (up to 6 at any one time) to run together.
